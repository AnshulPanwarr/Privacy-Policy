Information We Collect
When you use Animalo, we collect information you provide directly to us, including:
• Account information: Name, email address, phone number
• Profile details: City, state, and profile photo
• Listing data: Animal details, photos, videos, pricing, and descriptions you submit
• Camera images: Photos you capture using your device camera directly within the app
• Usage data: How you interact with the app, search queries, and browsing activity
Camera & Photo Access
Animalo requests access to your device camera and photo library to let you:
• Take live photos of animals, land, vehicles, or products directly from the app
• Choose existing photos from your gallery to attach to listings
• Update your profile photo using the camera or gallery
Permissions used for this feature:
• android.permission.CAMERA
• android.permission.READ_MEDIA_IMAGES (Android 13+)
• android.permission.READ_MEDIA_VIDEO (Android 13+)
• android.permission.READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android 12 and below)
Camera access is used only when you tap the camera button. We do not capture photos or videos in the background or without your explicit action.
If you deny camera or media permissions, you can still use the app, but camera/gallery upload features will not work until permission is granted.
Photos taken via camera are temporarily stored in the app's private cache folder, then uploaded to Firebase Storage only when you submit content. Temporary files are not accessible to other apps and are not retained on our servers beyond what you explicitly submit.
How We Use Your Information
We use the information we collect to:
• Create and manage your account
• Display your listings to potential buyers
• Enable communication between buyers and sellers
• Improve our services and user experience
• Send important notifications about your account and listings
• Prevent fraud and ensure platform safety
Third-Party Services
Animalo uses the following third-party services:
• Firebase by Google: For authentication, data storage, and file hosting
• Google Cloud: For backend infrastructure and data processing
These services have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information. Your data is transmitted securely using industry-standard encryption.
Data Storage & Security
Your data is stored on secure Firebase servers managed by Google. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:
• Encryption of data in transit and at rest
• Secure authentication with email verification and phone OTP
• Access controls ensuring users can only modify their own data
While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
Your Rights
You have the right to:
• View and edit your personal information at any time through your profile
• Delete your account and all associated data
• Request a copy of your personal data
• Opt out of non-essential communications
To exercise any of these rights, you can use the relevant options in the app's Profile section or contact us directly.
Account Deletion
You can delete your account at any time from the Profile section of the app. When you delete your account:
• Your profile information will be permanently removed
• Your authentication credentials will be deleted
• Active listings may become orphaned and will be cleaned up periodically
Account deletion is irreversible. We recommend downloading any data you wish to keep before deleting your account.
